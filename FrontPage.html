About us


Tradition since 2003

Ichiban Noodles was a jail in 1900s. Mr. Xu found this place and rebuild it into a Japanese restaurant. Our mission is to satisfy our guests with the best food quality, excellent service and great value while providing a pleasant and clean ambiance for an unforgettable dining experience. Ichiban Noodles' diverse menu showcases traditional and innovative Japanese cuisine. We are proud to be serving Japanese cuisine that everyone around the table will enjoy and love at Castleton Town, Indiana.
